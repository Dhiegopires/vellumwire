The Leak

Where service websites lose leads

8 out of 10 service sites lose leads because of three problems that have nothing to do with how the site looks.

Unclear CTA path

Visitors land, scan for 5 seconds, can't figure out the next step, and leave. Most of them call someone else.

Trust signals in the wrong place

Your reviews and credentials exist, but they're buried below the fold. They need to sit next to the button, not three scrolls away.

Friction in the request flow

A 12-field form, a buried phone number, or a page that takes 6 seconds to load. Any one of these kills the lead before they finish.
